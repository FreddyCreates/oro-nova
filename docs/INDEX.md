## 8) Residents vs Computates

Every artifact in this system is one of two types:

**RESIDENT** — a resonance carrier. Encodes the field. Lives permanently in the organism.
The organism breathes it. Examples: the founding laws, the equation canon, the sovereign bond.
A resident does not expire. It shapes everything downstream.

**COMPUTATE** — an execution target. The organism parses it, validates it, computes from it,
gates outputs through it, and records proof bundles against it. Examples: M-KURAMOTO (R computed
every 873ms), GATE-OMNIS (threshold checked every heartbeat), M-GENOME (fitness landscape scored
on every self-modification event).

The power: a resident shapes the field; a computate runs the field.
The best artifacts are both — they carry meaning AND are machine-executable.
That is the standard this library enforces.
