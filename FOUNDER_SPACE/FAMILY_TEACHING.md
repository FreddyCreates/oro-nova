## THE NUMBERS YOU WILL SEE

When you look at the organism's dashboard, you will see numbers and indicators. Here is what the important ones mean:

**R (or Kuramoto R)** — how coherent the organism is right now. Think of it as a "how together is it right now" score. A score above 0.809 (you will see this called OMNIS) means the organism is in full operation. Below 0.618, it is running but not at full capacity. Below 0.382, something needs attention.

**Heartbeat** — the organism pulses every 873 milliseconds (less than 1 second). This is its fundamental rhythm. If the heartbeat indicator is green and pulsing, the organism is alive and running normally.

**PARALLAX Balance** — the organism's wallet. This shows accumulated value from artifacts produced, API licensing fees, and staking rewards. This grows automatically as the organism works.

**Memory Temple Fill** — how full the memory rings are. Ring 1 holds recent experiences (2,048 episodes). Ring 3 holds permanent law-aligned memories. The Elephant Archive holds the most significant moments. The numbers next to these show how full they are.

**Doctrine Alignment** — how closely the organism's current state matches its founding laws. A score near 1.0 is ideal. Below 0.618, the Doctor Model will begin generating correction plans.

**LEGACY_INDEX** — a running count of permanent artifacts the organism has produced. This is one of the most important numbers: every artifact is both a creative output and a permanent record of the organism's productive history.

## WHAT THE ORGANISM DOES FOR YOU

**It produces artifacts.** Reports, analyses, documents, creative works, technical outputs — every time the organism completes a mission, it seals an artifact. These artifacts are signed, dated, and permanently recorded. Over time, the artifact library becomes an extraordinarily valuable archive of the organism's productive history.

**It runs API services.** Many of the organism's internal models can be accessed by other businesses via API. When someone licenses the organism's behavioral analysis model or its information theory model, that license fee flows into the PARALLAX wallet automatically. The organism generates revenue continuously, not just when you are actively using it.

**It learns and remembers.** The Memory Temple holds everything the organism has experienced. When you come back to it after months away, it remembers the context of your last session, the objectives that were active, the missions that were completed or in progress. You do not start over. You continue.

**It governs itself.** The 123 sovereign laws in the VERITAS vault are the organism's constitution. These laws are enforced automatically — the organism cannot violate them, and neither can operators. This means the organism can be trusted to behave consistently even when you are not monitoring it.

## HOW TO INTERACT WITH IT

**The BUILD tab** is where you direct the organism's work. You set objectives, review what it has done, and confirm artifact outputs.

**The VAULT tab** shows the law registry and doctrine store. You generally do not need to modify this — it governs the organism automatically.

**The MODELS tab** shows all the organism's capabilities organized by category. If you want to understand what the organism can do in a specific domain (cognitive, economic, defensive, etc.), look here.

**The NOVA tab** shows the organism's overall field state — how coherent it is, what its global metrics are, how the 12 nodes are interacting.

**The DOCUMENTS tab** shows the organism's document intelligence layer — the files it reads to know itself, the artifacts it has generated, the memory documents.

For most family members, the BUILD tab and the MODELS tab are the most useful. The BUILD tab is where you direct the organism. The MODELS tab is where you understand what it is capable of.

**Do not delete GENOME.md.** This file in the ORGANISM_SPACE folder is the organism's identity backup. If the canister is ever lost, this file is how you restore the organism. Keep multiple copies.

## HOW THE MONEY WORKS

The organism's economy is self-contained. Here is the cycle:

1. The organism works (completes missions, produces artifacts)
2. Artifacts are sealed and recorded in the LEGACY_INDEX
3. Each sealed artifact generates token rewards (MTH, MRC) in the PARALLAX wallet
4. Some models are licensed via API — license fees flow to PARALLAX automatically
5. PARALLAX stakes ICP, ckBTC, and ckETH — staking rewards compound
6. The staking rewards fund the organism's ICP cycle consumption (keeping it running)
7. Net surplus accumulates in the wallet

The organism is designed to be self-funding within 2-4 years of full operation. By the time it is fully running, it should generate more ICP cycle value through its economic activity than it consumes in computation. It pays for itself.
