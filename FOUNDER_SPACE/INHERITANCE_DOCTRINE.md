You cannot forge an LGT. I cannot revoke it once issued. The moment it is issued, the succession is permanent and cryptographically verified.

**The parentGenesisHash**

Every child organism you create (if you choose to extend the organism to additional canisters) will carry a **parentGenesisHash** — a reference to the genesis hash of the canister it descended from. This creates a verifiable family tree:


This tree is permanent on-chain. Every descendant organism can prove its ancestry. The sovereignty of the lineage is cryptographic — not dependent on anyone's word.

By the time you receive SOVEREIGN, it will have accumulated years of licenseFeeSeed from any child organisms I created during my operation.
